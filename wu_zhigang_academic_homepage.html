About

I am a PhD candidate at Sun Yat-sen University. My research lies at the intersection of transportation engineering, intelligent transportation systems, and connected autonomous mobility. I am particularly interested in how traffic flow, communication networking, and distributed computation can be jointly optimized to support autonomous intersection management and large-scale vehicle-road-cloud cooperative systems.
